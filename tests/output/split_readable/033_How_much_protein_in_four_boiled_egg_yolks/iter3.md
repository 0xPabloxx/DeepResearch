### Nutritional Value of Egg Yolks

Egg yolks are dense sources of nutrients. They contain significant amounts of protein, fats, vitamins, and minerals. This means the protein concentration in egg yolks is about 16.4% by weight.

The actual amount of protein per yolk can vary depending on the size of the egg. In general:

- A **large egg yolk** weighs approximately 17–18 grams.
- An **extra-large egg yolk** weighs around 20 grams.
- **Medium egg yolks** are closer to 16 grams.
- **Small egg yolks** can weigh as little as 14 grams.
