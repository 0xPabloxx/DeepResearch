## Overview of the Ganges–Brahmaputra–Meghna (GBM) River System

### Discharge and Significance

The Ganges–Brahmaputra–Meghna system is among the largest in the world due to the merging of three significant rivers:
- **Ganges**: Originating in India’s Himalayas, this river is revered in Hinduism and is critical for agriculture, drinking water, and industrial supply in northern and eastern India.
