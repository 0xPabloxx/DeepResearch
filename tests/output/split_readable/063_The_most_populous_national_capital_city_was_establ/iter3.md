- **Original Settlement**: The earliest significant settlement occurred in 1457 with the construction of Edo Castle.
- **Political Center**: Edo became the seat of the shogunate in 1603.
- **Modern Capital**: The transformation from Edo to Tokyo occurred in 1868, when it officially became the imperial capital of Japan.

Despite these layers of history, most contemporary sources refer to the year 1868 as the founding or establishment date for Tokyo as a national capital city. This is especially true in governmental records, city infoboxes, and historical accounts that highlight Tokyo’s emergence as the administrative and symbolic heart of Japan.
