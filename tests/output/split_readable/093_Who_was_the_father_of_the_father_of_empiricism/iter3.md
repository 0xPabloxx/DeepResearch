To directly answer the question: **If “the father of empiricism” is regarded as Francis Bacon, then “the father of the father of empiricism” is Sir Nicholas Bacon**, the Lord Keeper of the Great Seal under Elizabeth I. However, given the general usage of the term, especially in historical and scientific contexts, the most accurate answer is:

> **Sir Nicholas Bacon** was the father of Francis Bacon, who is commonly credited as the father of empiricism.
