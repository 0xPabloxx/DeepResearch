However, even moderate death totals for a storm of Wilma’s magnitude underscore the continuing dangers posed by hurricanes, especially in coastal and island communities. Each additional rise in category intensity significantly increases the risk of destruction, emphasizing the importance of ongoing research and investment in disaster prevention and emergency management.
