When considering "the longest running talk show," it's important to clarify what is meant by "talk show." Generally, this term refers to television programs where a host engages guests in conversation, often combining humor, interviews, entertainment news, and sometimes musical performances or sketches. "The Tonight Show" fits this definition well, having been on the air since 1954 and becoming an iconic American institution.

### History and Significance of The Tonight Show

"The Tonight Show" premiered in 1954 with Steve Allen as its first host. Over the decades, it has had several hosts, each bringing their own style to the show:

| Host                | Years Aired      |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| Steve Allen         | 1954–1957       |
| Jack Paar           | 1957–1962       |
| Johnny Carson       | 1962–1992       |
| Jay Leno            | 1992–2009, 2010–2014 |
| Conan O'Brien       | 2009–2010       |
| Jimmy Fallon        | 2014–present    |

By 2016, Jimmy Fallon had been hosting "The Tonight Show" since February 2014, following a highly publicized transition from previous host Jay Leno. The show aired live every weeknight from Studio 6B at NBC Studios in New York City.

### Why Is The Tonight Show Considered the Longest Running Talk Show?

"The Tonight Show" is recognized as the longest-running network late-night talk show in the United States due to its continuous run since 1954. It pioneered the late-night talk format on American television and influenced countless other shows that followed. Although there are older radio talk shows (such as BBC's "Desert Island Discs," which began in 1942), these are typically categorized as radio programs rather than television talk shows.

While there are some news-based programs like "Meet the Press" (which debuted in 1947) that have existed longer, they are generally classified separately due to their focus on news analysis and political discussion instead of general entertainment and celebrity interviews.

### Jimmy Fallon’s Tenure

Jimmy Fallon brought a new energy and digital savvy to "The Tonight Show" when he took over as host. His tenure is characterized by viral video segments, interactive games, modern musical choices, and a focus on inclusivity. By 2016, Fallon was firmly established as the host shaping the show's contemporary identity.

### Broader Context: Other Notable Talk Shows

Other prominent American talk shows that were running in 2016 include:

- "Live with Kelly and Ryan" – daytime variety.
- "The Late Late Show with James Corden" – starting in 2015.
- "The Daily Show with Trevor Noah" – satirical news commentary.
- "Good Morning America" – morning news/talk hybrid.

None of these shows have the same continuous history as "The Tonight Show," which sets the standard for longevity and cultural influence in the genre.

### International Considerations

If we broaden the definition to include non-television formats or consider global radio programs, then "Desert Island Discs" (UK) would hold the record as one of the longest-running interview-style programs. However, even in the UK context, most people refer to television talk shows when using the term "talk show."

### Conclusion

Therefore, based on standard definitions and prevailing public perception, "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon" was the longest running talk show in the US and internationally recognized as such in 2016. Jimmy Fallon was the host during that year, continuing the legacy of a program that has shaped American entertainment for over six decades.
