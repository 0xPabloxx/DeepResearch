## Connection to "Carol of the Bells"

“Carol of the Bells” is based on “Shchedryk,” a Ukrainian folk song that Leontovych collected and arranged into a choral piece. While the original lyrics referenced themes of generosity and blessing for the coming year, Leontovych’s arrangement transformed the folk tune into something that would resonate globally.

His arrangement of "Shchedryk" gained international acclaim after being performed abroad and later adapted with English lyrics. The powerful rhythm and haunting melody became widely recognized as the basis for the modern "Carol of the Bells," celebrated during the Christmas season worldwide.
